About Buddy

Buddy is a friendly and energetic Golden Retriever who loves to play fetch and go for long walks. He's great with children and other dogs, making him perfect for an active family. Buddy is house-trained and knows basic commands like sit, stay, and come.

He was rescued from a high-kill shelter three months ago and has been thriving in our care. Buddy is looking for a forever home with a family who can give him plenty of exercise and attention.

Personality

Friendly Energetic Loyal Intelligent Playful Good with kids Good with other dogs

Medical History

  • Neutered
  • Vaccinations up to date
  • Microchipped
  • Regular check-ups completed
  • No known health issues

Care Requirements

  • Regular exercise (at least 1 hour daily)
  • Grooming: Brushing 2-3 times weekly
  • Diet: High-quality dog food, 2 cups twice daily
  • Needs a secure yard or regular walks
  • Thrives on mental stimulation and training

Adoption Fee

$250 (includes neutering, vaccinations, microchip, and initial supplies)
